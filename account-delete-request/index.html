Account Delete Request

RuBaRu is a Fully OnChain Content Creator-Consumer platform, ensuring that users have complete ownership of their accounts, including the ability to delete their profiles from the platform. The steps below detail the process to request the deletion of a user account from the platform.

  • Compose Deletion Request Email:

    Open your registered email account and compose an email requesting the deletion of your decentralized social media account

  • Use Registered Email Address:

    Ensure that you send the deletion request email from the email address associated with your social media account for verification purposes

  • Subject Line:

    Clearly state the purpose of your email in the subject line, such as "Account Deletion Request"

  • Provide Account Details:

    In the body of the email, include relevant account details to verify your identity. This may include your username, registered email address, and any other information requested by the platform.

  • Express Intent to Delete Account:

    Clearly state your intent to delete your account. Be concise and direct in your language.

  • Reason (Optional):

    Optionally, you may provide a brief reason for your decision to delete the account. Some platforms may request feedback to improve their services.

  • Request Confirmation:

    Request confirmation of your account deletion and specify any additional steps or information required from your end.

  • Check for Platform-Specific Instructions:

    Review the platform's account deletion policies for any specific instructions or requirements. Some platforms may have a dedicated email address or specific guidelines for account deletion requests.

  • Send Email:

    Once you have composed the email and attached any necessary information, send it to the "admin@rubaru.app" or follow any specific instructions outlined by the platform.

  • Monitor Inbox for Confirmation:

    Keep an eye on your email inbox for any confirmation or further instructions from the platform regarding your account deletion request.

  • Follow-Up (if necessary):

    If there is no response or if you encounter any issues, consider following up with the platform's customer support through the email address provided in their support documentation.

Remind users to exercise caution and ensure they are complying with the platform's policies when requesting account deletion through email. Additionally, inform them that the processing time for account deletion requests may vary.
